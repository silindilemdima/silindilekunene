Common Questions

Questions
worth asking.

If you don't see your question here, just reach out directly — I respond to every inquiry personally.

Ask a Question
How do payments work? +
I work on a milestone-based payment structure. A 50% deposit is required to start the project, with the remaining 50% due upon final delivery. For larger projects, we can agree on a custom payment schedule split across key milestones.
Do you work with clients outside South Africa? +
Yes — I work with clients globally. Invoicing for international clients is done in USD or ZAR depending on preference. I've worked with clients across the UK, Nigeria, Kenya, the UAE, and the US. Time zone differences are managed through structured async communication and scheduled video calls.
How many revision rounds are included? +
The number of revision rounds depends on the package. Starter includes 2 rounds, Growth includes 3 per phase, and Premium includes unlimited revisions. In practice, most projects are resolved well within the included rounds — because I invest heavily in discovery upfront so we're aligned before any design begins.
What files will I receive at the end? +
You receive a complete, organised file package including: AI and EPS source files, SVG files, high-resolution PNG and PDF exports (in all colour variants), web-optimised assets, and the full brand guidelines document as a print-ready PDF. Everything you need to use your brand correctly across any application.
Can you help if I only need a small update? +
Yes — for smaller, focused projects I offer a day-rate of R3,500 or a half-day rate of R2,000. This works well for things like updating an existing brand asset, designing a single marketing piece, or reviewing and refreshing specific brand materials. Reach out and we'll find the right structure.
What makes you different from other designers? +
I bring three things most designers don't: strategic thinking before any visual work, deep knowledge of African brand culture and markets, and senior-level craft across every medium. I'm also genuinely invested in outcomes — not just deliverables. My repeat client rate is over 80%, which tells you something about how I work.
How far in advance should I book? +
I take a limited number of projects per quarter to protect quality. I'm typically booked 4–8 weeks in advance. If you have a specific launch date in mind, the earlier you reach out, the better. I always recommend a discovery call first — no obligation — so we can confirm fit and timeline before committing.
