Full-time Creative Roles
I grew up in Durban watching my mother rearrange furniture at 2am, convinced a different layout would change the feeling of the room. He was right. That obsession with how space communicates feeling became the foundation of everything I do.
"I don't design logos. I design the first impression a brand will ever make."
I studied graphic design at VEGA School in Johannesburg, where I discovered that the best design is invisible — it communicates everything without announcing itself. After graduating, I joined Grid & Co., one of Johannesburg's most respected brand studios, where I spent four years learning how to translate business strategy into visual language.
In 2019, I went independent. Not because I was dissatisfied — but because I wanted to go deeper. When you work for yourself, every decision matters more. Every pixel is a choice you own completely. I took on fewer projects and gave each one more of myself.
Today my studio works with brands across South Africa and internationally — from luxury startups finding their identity, to established companies redefining who they are. I specialise in brand identity, web design, and creative direction. What I'm really building, in every project, is trust between a brand and its audience.
When I'm not designing, I'm at the Neighbourgoods Market with an iced coffee, watching how people move through spaces. Everything is reference. Everything is research.

Design Philosophy

How I think
about great
design.

These aren't rules — they're instincts refined by 8 years of client work, late nights, and the particular satisfaction of watching something complicated become beautifully simple.

01
Restraint is a decision.
Every element you remove is as intentional as every element you add. White space isn't emptiness — it's breathing room for meaning.
02
Strategy before aesthetics.
Beautiful design that doesn't communicate the right thing is decoration. I start with the why before I touch the how.
03
Culture is context.
African design doesn't need to imitate Western luxury to be premium. Our context is rich, complex, and deeply beautiful. I design from inside it.
04
Typography carries emotion.
The right typeface can make a brand feel trustworthy, joyful, or authoritative before a single word is read. Type is tone.
05
Consistency builds trust.
A brand identity that behaves the same at every touchpoint — across media, scale, and time — is a brand people believe in.
