About Me

Design as a
language of
intention.

I'm Silindile Kunene — a multidisciplinary creative based in Johannesburg, South Africa. I specialize in graphic design, brand identity, web design, and digital experiences that merge strategy with visual artistry.

With 8+ years in the creative industry, I've worked with startups, established corporations, and cultural brands across Africa and globally — crafting identities that don't just look good, but communicate with precision and purpose.

My approach is rooted in deep research, bold conceptual thinking, and meticulous execution. Every project begins with understanding the story, and ends with design that tells it unforgettably.

My Process

How great
design happens.

Every project follows a disciplined creative process — not rigid, but rigorous. This is how ideas become identities, and concepts become experiences that last.

01
Discover & Define
Deep-dive research into your brand, competitors, audience, and market. We align on goals, vision, and what success actually looks like.
02
Concept & Explore
Moodboards, creative territories, and initial directions. Three conceptual paths, each with a distinct visual language and strategic rationale.
03
Design & Refine
We develop the chosen direction to its fullest — every detail considered, every application tested. Revision rounds until it's perfect.
04
Deliver & Guide
Complete deliverable package with all files, brand guidelines, and a handover session ensuring you know exactly how to use your new identity.
